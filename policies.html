Return Policy

US Returns/ Exchanges

* The below applies to US customers only

Returns at C Me Sexy!

1. MAKE SURE YOUR ITEM(s) QUALIFY FOR RETURN
To qualify for return your item must be in its original, unworn condition, free of perfume, smoke or any other odor with all tags still attached for up to 10 days from the date of purchase. On sale items and undergarments cannot be returned.  Shoes must be returned un-scuffed in an additional, separate, packaging box and with any cushioning material that arrived inside the shoe box still inside the shoe box.

2. EXCHANGES
If you would like to exchange your style for another style or size please contact us via email.

Please allow approximately 3 to 4 weeks from the date your item(s) is mailed back for us to process it and email you. Shipping is non-refundable. In most cases the refund will be reflected in your balance within a few days of processing but depending upon your credit card company's processing speed it may take them up to one full billing cycle (one month) to adjust your balance to reflect your refund.
